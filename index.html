RGB Switcher

Swipe to survive in a dying black-and-white world. Use RGB color powers to fight enemies, unlock abilities, and restore light in this fast-paced roguelike adventure.

About us

Szenos Games is a Hungarian one-man indie game studio creating engaging gaming experiences for mobile phones. Our mission is simple: to prove that mobile games can offer deep, immersive worlds just like their console counterparts.
