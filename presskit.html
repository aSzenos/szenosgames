About the Game:

RGB Switcher is a fast-paced roguelike adventure where players use 3 switches to mix colors.

By doing this, they can restore color to a dying world. Players encounter enemies, and unlock new abilities.

The game combines roguelike elements with swipe-based controls and color mixing, offering a unique and engaging gameplay experience.
